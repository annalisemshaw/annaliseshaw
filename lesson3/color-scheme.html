Color Scheme

Color #1

#EEEEE

The first color is a basic white for the background. It is also the color of the nav links until they're hovered over.

Color #2

#222831

The text will be black throughout the logo and body. The nav links when hovered over will turn black.

Color #3

#4F8A8B

The green is an accent color used in the navigation bar. I am using it to give some contrast to the page.

Color #4

#FBD46D

The header and footer are a warm yellow just like the yellow used in the logo. I wanted there to be unity between the logo and colors of the elements on the page.
