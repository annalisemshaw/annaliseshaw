Target Audience

Who is this site for anyways?

My site is for athletes who need to know what the weather conditions will be like on days where they will have to be outside! They need to know if they need to bring a jacket just in case there's a chance of rain. Or if the weather is not suitable for outdoor activities, they can be prepared for possible cancellations.

Personas

Persona #1:

Jordan is a cross country runner who competes on her high school cross country team. She likes to get extra practice in on the weekend.

Scenario: Jordan likes to wake up early and go for a run. But she doesn't know what to wear. Should she dress light or should she add some layers? She solves this predicament by searching up the weather conditions on this site.

Scenario: Jordan has a tournament tomorrow and needs to know if the weather will impact the scheduling of the tournament.

Scenario: Jordan just bought new shoes with more grip for tougher terrain. She wants to break them in. She can come to this site to see if the weather is suitable to break her new shoes in.

Persona #2:

Eliezer is a soccer coach. He has to not only coach but he sends out the announcements regarding games and practice.

Scenario: Eliezer has a soccer game tommorrow and needs to see if there are any changes in the weather. This way he can let his team know that the game will be cancelled due to rain.

Scenario: Eliezer wants to book a retreat for his team. He wants to see what the weather will be like where the resort is located.
